Privacy Policy for Lemur Express 11 Employee App

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

This app is designed for internal restaurant operations and collects minimal data necessary for functionality:

  • Employee login credentials (username/password)
  • Order management data (order status updates, timestamps)
  • Device information for app functionality
  • Usage analytics for app improvement

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely for:

  • Employee authentication and access control
  • Order management and kitchen workflow
  • App performance monitoring
  • Providing customer service and support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal information

5. Employee Rights

Employees have the right to:

  • Access their personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of personal data (subject to business requirements)

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for business operations and legal requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for employees 18+ and does not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Employees will be notified of significant changes.
